        <h3 class="publication-title">NavSpace: Evaluating Spatial Intelligence of Vision-Language Navigation Agents</h3>
        <p class="publication-authors">
            <span class="highlight">Haolin Yang</span>, [Co-authors TBD]
        </p>
        <p class="publication-venue">In Preparation, 2025</p>
        <p class="project-description">
            We present NavSpace, a comprehensive benchmark designed to evaluate the spatial intelligence of navigation agents. Our benchmark covers six major categories of spatial reasoning abilities: distance estimation, direction understanding, object localization, spatial relationships, route planning, and landmark recognition. Through extensive experiments with state-of-the-art VLN models, we reveal significant gaps in current agents' spatial understanding capabilities.
