NavSpace: How Navigation Agents Follow Spatial Intelligent Instructions
Haolin Yang*, Yuxing Long*, Zhuoyuan Yu, Zihan Yang, Minghan Wang, Jiapeng Xu, Yihan Wang, Ziyan Yu, Wenzhe Cai, Lei Kang, Hao Dong†
The first benchmark about spatial intelligence in embodied navigation
Preprint, 2025  
arXiv

We introduce the NavSpace benchmark with six task categories and 1,228 trajectory-instruction pairs to evaluate spatial intelligence in navigation agents. We comprehensively evaluate 22 agents and propose SNav, a spatially intelligent navigation model that outperforms existing approaches.
